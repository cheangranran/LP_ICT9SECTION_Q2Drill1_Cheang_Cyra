BITCOIN

Bitcoin is an invention that uses cryptography to create a new form of money called a digital commodity.


The invention of bitcoin is famously attributed to “Satoshi Nakamoto,” a pseudonym used by the author of the bitcoin white paper published in 2008.

TETHER

Tether was founded by group of Bitcoin enthusiasts and early adopters


Tether has created a revolutionary building block that companies worldwide can use to offer democratised cross-border financial services. Tether tokens are referred to as stablecoins because they offer price stability as they are pegged to a fiat currency.

RIPPLE

Ripple is one of the largest cryptocurrency platforms by market capitalization.Ripple Labs, the company behind Ripple and its native cryptocurrency token XRP, had been around since 2012


Ripple was first founded back in 2004 as RipplePay in Vancouver, Canada by Ryan Fugger as a way of securely moving money around the world.

ETHEREUM

Ethereum is the second-largest cryptocurrency by market cap, but its more than just digital money.


Launched in 2015 by Vitalik Buterin , Ethereum gives developers tools to build and run a variety of applications. These apps are called decentralised applications, or dApps.
